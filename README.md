The **System** was developed to improve the control and management of technical service requests in an industrial environment.  
The solution centralizes service information, organizes workflow, and allows accurate tracking of response time, service time, and technician waiting time. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Additionally, the system supports ticket prioritization by production line, failure tracking, and automated report generation to support decision-making. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Problems Addressed

Before this solution, the following challenges were identified:

- Lack of control over technical service requests  
- Difficulty calculating service time  
- Difficulty tracking technician waiting time  
- Lack of failure management in production lines  
- Difficulty generating reports  
- Lack of alignment regarding equipment downtime :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Solution Goals

The system aims to provide:

- Centralized information management  
- Ticket time tracking  
- Improved service management  
- Ticket prioritization  
- Automated report generation  
- Failure tracking and root cause analysis :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

## System Benefits

### Operational Team
- Maintenance ticket tracking  
- Technician waiting time monitoring  
- Easy creation of failure reports  

### Technical Team
- Ticket prioritization  
- Optimized service workflow  
- Easier shift handovers  
- Service history tracking  
- Troubleshooting support  

### Analysts and Management
- Preventive maintenance prioritization  
- Failure report management  
- Data-driven improvement prioritization  
- Technical performance evaluation  
- Resource and equipment management :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## System Workflow

The system follows these steps:

1. Ticket creation by the operational team  
2. Ticket visualization by the technical team  
3. Prioritization and service execution  
4. Time alignment between teams  
5. Data analysis and history generation by maintenance management :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
